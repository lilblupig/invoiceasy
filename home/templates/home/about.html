About Us

Who are we?

Well, at the moment "we", is actually just "I". I am an ex-accountant, new developer. My family are all in trades of some form, mostly self-employed, most now retired! My Dad is an electrician and pretty tech savvy, and he really has to do battle with his motivation levels to get on and do his billing. He has a template setup that he puts the details on, but like all of us, he sometimes overwrites the template, or a previously completed bill. Then, after a couple months of procrastinating about bills the printer won't print and needs 7 head cleans, after which it then declares it has no ink despite the test page looking totally fine! Aiiiiiiiiiii! If you don't know this exasperation, I envy you. It got me to thinking there must be a better way.

What is InvoicEasy?

It does what it says on the tin really. You sign up for a subscription, enter up your business info, add your clients, then bung your invoicing details into our forms, and when you are ready, a PDF is created for you to view and email as an attachment to your customer.

What do we do?

We charge as little as we can to cover hosting costs, and we hope that as the platform grows, we can reduce charges. We maintain the site and provide tech support, and the odd bit of fixing here and there. We might look at adding new features, but our target market of the micro-business will always be at the forefront in any new things we choose to explore.
